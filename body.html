Film and theatre can deeply affect individuals on an emotional, intellectual, and psychological level.
They provide a platform for storytelling and artistic expression that can evoke a wide range of emotions,
from joy and laughter to sadness and empathy. Through relatable characters and thought-provoking narratives,
these mediums can help people explore complex human experiences, encouraging self-reflection and personal growth.
Moreover, film and theatre can serve as a form of escapism, offering audiences an opportunity to step out of
their own lives and immerse themselves in different worlds, cultures, and time periods. This can expand one's
horizons, increase empathy, and contribute to a more profound understanding of the human condition.
